Our Menu

Every Dish Is Prepared With Care, Tradition, and The Bold Flavors of Nigeria.

🍚 Rice Dishes

Jollof Rice

A rich, smoky West African classic cooked in a seasoned tomato base and served with tender protein of your choice.

Fried Rice

Perfectly stir-fried with fresh vegetables, herbs, and spices for a flavorful yet comforting dish.

Ofada Rice & Ayamase

Local Ofada rice served with spicy green pepper sauce and assorted meats, slow-cooked for deep traditional flavor.

Coconut Rice

Fragrant rice cooked in fresh coconut milk and mild spices, offering a rich aroma and naturally creamy taste.

🍲 Swallow & Traditional Soups

Pounded Yam & Egusi Soup

Smooth pounded yam paired with thick melon-seed soup prepared with assorted meats and leafy greens.

Amala & Ewedu

Soft amala served with silky ewedu soup, blended with locust beans and traditional seasoning.

Eba & Okra Soup

Cassava-based eba served with fresh okra soup, cooked to a perfect balance of texture and taste.

🍖 Grills & Specialties

Suya Platter

Thinly sliced, flame-grilled beef coated in traditional suya spice, served with fresh onions and tomatoes.

Grilled Fish (Nigerian Style)

Whole fish marinated in pepper sauce and grilled to perfection, served with spicy accompaniments.

Grilled Chicken (Pepper Style)

Tender chicken marinated in house pepper sauce and flame-grilled for a bold, smoky flavor.

Barbecue Beef Ribs

Slow-cooked beef ribs glazed with rich barbecue sauce, finished on the grill for a deep, savory taste.

🍵 Light Meals & Sides

Pepper Soup

Moi Moi

Steamed bean pudding blended with peppers, onions, and spices, soft, rich, and satisfying.

Yam Fries with Pepper Sauce

Crispy golden yam fries served with a side of spicy house pepper sauce.
